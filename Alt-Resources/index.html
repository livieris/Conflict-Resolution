Mission Statement:

Assisting individuals and organizations to manage communication, conflict and change in healthy, constructive ways.



Honoring the value and dignity of each person served and understanding the importance of individual empowerment are Alternative Resolutions' top priorities.

Established in 1995 in Wisconsin, Alternative Resolutions, Inc. continues to be a leader in developing and providing innovative communication and conflict management services to organizations and individuals tailored to their specific needs. We have a broad range of experience in the workplace, business, court, school, medical, government, church, community, and family (including divorce) settings. Thousands of people have successfully turned conflict into opportunities for improved relationships, greater understanding, and increased productivity with the help of Alternative Resolutions. Our professional experience and expertise assure the highest quality services to help you achieve your goals.

What makes Alternative Resolutions, Inc. unique is that "one call does it all." We have the tools to handle all of your conflict needs, including mediation, facilitation, coaching, training, consultation, systems design and needs assessment. We know what works because we practice what we teach every day.

Alternative Resolutions is your conflict management specialist. Others say they are the best - we know we are. Make us prove it!
